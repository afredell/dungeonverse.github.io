Background

Gromack was born in 42 BK into a rugged and harsh orcish tribe, he quickly learned the ways of survival in the unforgiving wilderness. From a young age, Gromack displayed exceptional physical strength and combat skills, distinguishing himself among his peers.

During his childhood in 27 BK Gromack crossed paths with Throgmar, a skilled blacksmith dwarf. Throgmar's craftsmanship and dedication to his craft fascinated Gromack, and a deep friendship formed between the two. Gromack became fascinated with Throgmar's weapons, recognizing their superior quality and appreciating the reliability they provided in battles.

As they grew older, Gromack faced a perilous situation that tested his resolve. He found himself in the midst of a dangerous skirmish in 24 BK, outnumbered and on the brink of defeat. It was Throgmar who came to his aid, saving Gromack from certain demise. This act of bravery solidified their bond, and from that moment on, Gromack pledged himself as Throgmar's loyal bodyguard, vowing to protect him at all costs.

With Throgmar's weapons in hand, Gromack embarked on numerous adventures as a mercenary. His reputation as a formidable warrior and protector grew, and he became sought after by those in need of a steadfast guardian. Gromack's towering presence and unwavering dedication to his duties earned him respect among his peers and struck fear into the hearts of his adversaries.

Throughout his adult life, Gromack faced countless dangers and encountered a wide range of adversaries, from fearsome beasts of the wilderness to cunning humanoids. His unwavering loyalty to Throgmar remained unshaken, and he honed his combat skills with every battle, constantly seeking to improve and overcome any obstacles in their path.

Despite his intimidating appearance and serious demeanor, Gromack possessed a steadfast sense of honor and a protective nature towards those he cared about. Deeply loyal to his friends and companions, he became known for his unwavering commitment to their safety and well-being.Gromack stands a towering and formidable presence ready to face any challenge that comes his way. With Throgmar's expertly crafted weapons in his hands, Gromack is a force to be reckoned with, combining his raw strength, combat prowess, and undying loyalty to protect those he holds dear.

In 1 BK he joined the Journeybound Company to head westward toward Ohterlon. While with the company he was heavily relied on for protection of the wagons.

Personality

Despite his intimidating presence, Gromack possesses a deep sense of honor and loyalty. He is a serious and disciplined individual, driven by a strong moral compass and a desire to protect those he holds dear. Gromack is known for his unwavering commitment to his friends and allies, willing to put himself in harm's way to ensure their safety.

While Gromack may appear gruff and unapproachable to outsiders, those who earn his trust find a steadfast and dependable friend. He values honesty, integrity, and loyalty above all else and expects the same from those he associates with. Gromack's serious demeanor is often mistaken for coldness, but his closest friends know that beneath the hardened exterior lies a fierce protector and a compassionate heart.

With his wealth of experience and battle-hardened skills, Gromack has become a seasoned veteran, sought after for his expertise and unwavering dedication to the cause. He stands as a steadfast guardian, ready to face any challenge that comes his way, and is determined to continue fighting for justice and protecting those in need.
