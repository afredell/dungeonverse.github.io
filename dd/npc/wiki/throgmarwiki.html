Background

>Born into a lineage of renowned blacksmiths in 54 BK, Throgmar was introduced to the craft at a young age. Under the watchful eye of his skilled parents, he honed his skills, learning the techniques and secrets of the trade. With each passing year, his craftsmanship improved, and his reputation as a talented blacksmith grew.

During his early adulthood in 27 BK Throgmar formed an unbreakable bond with the young Gromack , the serious up and coming Orc Mercenary. Despite their differences in size and temperament, they became inseparable friends, often seen exchanging stories and laughter in the forge. Throgmar recognized Gromack's potential as a warrior and took pride in crafting weapons specifically tailored to suit his friend's impressive strength and combat style.

As an adult, Throgmar embarked on his own adventures with Gromack, venturing beyond the confines of the forge. Along his journeys, he encountered various cultures and encountered diverse challenges, expanding his knowledge and broadening his horizons. He embraced every opportunity to learn from other craftsmen and artisans, constantly seeking to refine his skills and discover new techniques to incorporate into his craft.

Despite his wanderlust, Throgmar always returned to his beloved forge, where he found solace and purpose. He established a thriving business in 22 BK, catering to warriors, adventurers, and nobles alike, forging weapons and armor of exceptional quality and durability. His reputation as a great blacksmith spread far and wide, attracting customers from distant lands who sought the craftsmanship that only Throgmar could provide.

In addition to his talents as a blacksmith, Throgmar possessed a kind and compassionate heart. He readily offered his assistance to those in need, whether it was repairing a farmer's tools or crafting a special gift for a loved one. His generosity and willingness to help others earned him the respect and admiration of his community.

In 1 BK He joined the Journeybound Compnay to head out west to Ohterlon, in hopes of opening a brand new forge for settlers. While part of the group he crafted armor and weapons for the travelers

Personality

Throgmar is a jolly and cheerful Dwarf, known for his hearty laughter and a constant twinkle in his eyes. He possesses a genuine love for life and an infectious enthusiasm for his craft. Always eager to greet others with a warm smile, Throgmar radiates a positive energy that uplifts those around him.

His jovial nature makes him a joy to be around, and he easily forms friendships wherever he goes. He has a knack for lightening the mood and finding humor in even the direst of situations. Throgmar's laughter is contagious, and it brings a sense of camaraderie to any group he's a part of.

Despite his cheerful disposition, Throgmar is a dedicated and disciplined blacksmith. He takes immense pride in his craft and is always striving for perfection. His attention to detail and meticulousness ensure that each piece he forges is of the highest quality. Throgmar's passion for his work is evident in every swing of his hammer and every delicate touch he applies to his creations.

While Throgmar enjoys a good drink and revelry, he never loses sight of his responsibilities and commitments. He is trustworthy, dependable, and loyal to his friends and companions. He values friendship deeply and cherishes the bonds he has formed throughout his life.

In times of adversity, Throgmar's unwavering optimism shines through. He sees challenges as opportunities for growth and approaches them with a determined spirit. His resilience and positive mindset inspire those around him, providing comfort and encouragement when it's most needed.
